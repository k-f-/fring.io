Now

Life

📍 Chattanooga, TN

I've made several good friends since moving here and that's ultra rewarding. Content. Life is good.

Work

I'm fortunate to be able to work remotely in a Senior Data Engineer/Analyst role for a Fortune 100 healthcare company. Day to day, I'm able to answer vague questions with facts and that feels great. It isn't always massively interesting, but I've rekindled a love of data and my daily output impacts tens of thousands of co-workers and many millions of members. The fact that my co-workers are fantastic is a bonus.

Putting time in on a semantic Data-Lake has been a deep dive on understanding Health Care data-sets. Being in-part responsible for what will become the single source of truth is engaging.

Future

  • I'd like to use different tools. Microsoft is fine, Teams & PowerBI are very good, but in my heart I'm a FOSS guy: Terminals, Python, R, etc. are more interesting to me.
  • I do appriciate the modern need for specialization. However, I'd like to flex a life-time of wearing many hats.
  • A good deal more flexibility with working hours. I always deliver. Don't make me sit at a desk just because I need to not be afk. Life is way too short.
